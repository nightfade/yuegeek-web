关于我们

我们是来自百度、阿里、网易游戏等知名企业，分布在北京、杭州和深圳的8人团队。
我们因对互联网产品和技术共同的爱好聚在一起， 致力于提供最专业的互联网技术服务，助您打造高质量的互联网产品。

Matt Sears

Founder / Chief Executive Officer

Matt Sears was born and raised in Dayton, Ohio. He spent most of his childhood drawing and painting. He ventured into the digital world by earning a degree in computer science and business. He founded Littlelines way back in 2007 with a mission of combining his two passions: high-tech and aesthetically superb design. When he is not running Littlelines like a pro, he loves living in Seattle with his gal, listening to his vinyl record collection, and taking in the occasional beach.
